# Project Context: NayaGhar

## 📌 Vision
NayaGhar is a **Demand-First Real Estate Marketplace** that replaces the traditional "Search & Scroll" model with a "Post & Bid" architecture. It functions as the **"Upwork for Real Estate,"** where properties find buyers rather than buyers chasing ghost listings.

## 🏗️ Core Architecture (The "Pull" Model)
1. **The Brief (Demand):** A verified buyer posts a specific requirement (Location, Budget, Amenities, Timeline).
2. **The Bid (Supply):** Verified agents/sellers submit specific, currently available properties that match the brief.
3. **The Match:** The buyer receives a curated shortlist of high-intent, 100% available options.

## 🎯 Problems Solved
*   **Stale Listings:** Eliminates "Data Decay" because agents only pitch properties available *now*.
*   **Buyer Fatigue:** Removes the manual labor of filtering through thousands of irrelevant digital classifieds.
*   **Information Asymmetry:** Shifts power to the buyer by forcing sellers to compete on relevance and transparency.

## 🛠️ Key Entities
*   **Buyer:** Posts "Requirement Briefs."
*   **Agent/Seller:** Submits "Property Bids."
